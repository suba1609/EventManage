Smart City Summit

Date: January 26, 2026

Location: Chennai Trade Centre

The Smart City Summit brings together innovators, urban developers, policy makers, and technology providers to discuss the future of Chennai’s infrastructure. Key topics include AI in urban planning, smart mobility, energy-efficient buildings, and digital governance. This event will include live demonstrations, panel discussions, startup showcases, and citizen idea booths.
